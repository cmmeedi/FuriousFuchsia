M-D-H

The most asked question “what number is my R/T Fuchsia Challenger”? The R/T Fuchsia Challenger was not a numbered production (only the SRT8). So, in order to determine what number your R/T Fuchsia Challenger is, would be by the M-D-H. By submitting your M-D-H I would be able to establish the sequence of your Challengers build and the 0000/1127 for it. PLEASE HELP and submit yours today.
